# Sponsors of the CoSAI Open Project

OASIS Open and CoSAI thank the following organizations, who generously support the work, funding the development of the interoperability standards and open source tools that their organizations and customers depend on.  
Starred (&bigstar;) organizations have taken the additional step of signing the enterprise contributor license agreement and seating a representative on the Project Governing Board. Organizations marked with a &bigtriangledown; symbol support CoSAI as a premier sponsor.
